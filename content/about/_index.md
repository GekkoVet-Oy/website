## Our Story

GekkoVet was founded in Finland in 2018 with a clear mission: bring structured, evidence-based clinical reasoning to every veterinary professional, everywhere.

Over 8 years, our team of 30 DVMs has built the world's most comprehensive veterinary intelligence platform — covering nearly 1,000 conditions with 37,000+ symptom-diagnosis combinations, all grounded in 80,000+ pages of medical references.

## Milestones

- **2018** — GekkoVet founded in Finland. Development of clinical decision support platform begins.
- **2019** — Platform expands across Nordics; clinics validate time-saving and diagnostic accuracy.
- **2021** — Finalist at VMX Innovation Pitch Competition in Orlando, USA — first non-U.S. company selected for finals.
- **2022** — Awarded Tempo Funding by Business Finland. Runner-up at WSAVA Congress in Lisbon.
- **2023** — Strategic partnership with Royal Canin for emerging markets in Asia. Selected for Leap Venture Studio Cohort 8 (Mars Petcare).
- **2024** — Multilingual input support launched. Finalist in Animal Health Innovation (London). Best in Show Spotlight at Pet Connect USA (Los Angeles). Global rollout following Royal Canin pilot — 92% of vets report increased efficiency.
- **2025** — Speaking functionality released (voice → SOAP notes). Copilot Early Access launched, combining GekkoVet dataset with open-source AI.
